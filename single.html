Coney Island Sand Sculpting Contest

  • Super user
  • August 8th, 2016
  • Hits:145

The Coney Island Sand Sculpting Contest is a free family-friendly event open to amateurs and semi-professional sand sculpting artists of all ages competing for cash prizes and bragging rights.

Over the years, the Coney Island Sand Sculpting Contest has attracted thousands of attendees from around the world. This is a special year for our contest, commemorating the 150th Anniversary of Brooklyn Community Services.

Register for your chance to turn sand into a towering masterpiece.

Astella Development Corporation and Brooklyn Community Services have partnered to host this year's Coney Island Sand Sculpting Contest. All donations and sponsorships benefit individuals and families. Special thanks to the Coney Island staff of the NYC Dept. of Parks & Recreation for all their hard work in preparing the beach for the Sand Sculpting Contest.
